## How to sign

We use the CLA Assistant bot. When you open your first pull request, the bot
will post a comment with instructions. To sign, add a pull-request comment with
exactly:

Your signature (your GitHub username) is recorded so you only sign once. If a
Contribution is made on behalf of a company that owns your work, ensure you are
authorized before signing.
